Become a Member

Sevankur Member

Any undergraduate (M.B.B.S., B.D.S., B.A.M.S., B.H.M.S., B.O.Th., B.P.Th.) or postgraduate student, interns, super specialty students, can register as a member of Sevankur. Medical officers, teaching staff in medical colleges, private practitioners and even non-medicos can join this activity as Karyakartas. Sevankur is an entirely non-political organization connected with medical students and staff. Sevankur is open for all irrespective of any caste, creed, or religion.

There is no fee for registration as a member of Sevankur. No compulsion will be imposed on members registered with Sevankur. It is completely voluntary activity.

Why you should become a member?

Networking Opportunities: The greatest leaders learn from others. When you join an association, you’re instantly part of a team that’s greater than yourself.

Community Growth: When you become a member of an association, you’ll learn more than you ever imagined about ways to reach out to the community.

Inspiration: As a member of an association, you can go into each day knowing you’re not alone. You’ll have insights, resources, case studies.

Access to Education and Training: Most professional and trade associations provide a vast amount of education, workshops, trainings and conferences.
